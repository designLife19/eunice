Hi, my name is Eunice (Haeun) Kim

I am a UX / UI designer with a deep passion for understanding how technology impacts people’s behavior and influences their habits.

More about me

I’m who enjoys working with people in a fast-paced and detail-oriented environment.

I have 5 years of experience as a UX/ UI designer and 8 years of experience as a graphic designer.

I have been involved in various projects in UI/UX development. My main tasks were creating wireframes, storyboards, user flows, process flows, icons and illustrations to effectively communicate interaction and design ideas. And designing and developing interactive prototypes and mockups using Sketch, Illustrator, Photoshop, also created low to high-fidelity prototypes with HTML, CSS, and jQuery for future enhancements of the website. Also working with the development team to correct any UI problems or HTML/CSS issues. Furthermore, Created responsive web landing pages and e-charts, email template design and regular day-to-day design requirements.

I have developed a wide range of forward-thinking user interface software, which have all improved user experience, and this has aided my proficient knowledge in the process and responsibilities of this position. And I am comfortable working with a project based environment, working through a SDLC in Agile methodology, and defining and refining requirements from users and other stakeholders.

I would like to meet you to discuss my work experience. Please feel free to contact me in case you need more information or seek clarification.
