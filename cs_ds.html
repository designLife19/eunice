Overview

DrSongApp is the web and mobile app of Dr.song Square Academy. Dr.song Square Academy helps students improve their learning and skills. There are many courses from first graders to university students. The range of lectures is diverse and fragmented.

As the number of students grows, the client realized that they need to develop an management application that can manage teachers and students, manage all courses and reports. At the first meeting, the client required a new web management application for administrator and teachers. However, required to build responsive web / mobile version for only students.

The roles and goals are as follows:
1. Administrator - Managing all teachers, students, assign subjects and courses
2. Teachers - Managing topics and worksheets, assigned students
3. Students - Checking daily worksheets and update answers

I was involved this project as a 3-month contract UX / UI designer and front-end developer.

Challenges & Solving

Challenges

This project is new management application and I had to do UX / UI and Front-End coding alone within 12 weeks except for Back_End development.

To begin with, I met with company executives to understand the vision and plans for this project and the client asked to skip the research phase, and low-fidelity prototype phase altogether. Because the time is limited. Therefore, I focused on the resources I’ve got from the client and streamlined the process as much as possible such as using a paper survey to 5 teachers and 5 students.

Through the plenty of communication, I understood the goals, motivations, and pain points of the users within their servies as well as resolving any problems by designing a better solution for them.

There are some main points I shoud focus on;
  • Needed a cohesive structure, look, and feel between different catagories
  • The hierarchical structure of this application consists of an internal menu
And, The specific requirements of the user are listed below.
  • Users required no confusion in navigating each subject-course-worksheet
  • Users required to be easy to manage lectures or students assigned to them on the same page.
  • The client wants to provide mobile services for students to improve their education, no matter where they are.
